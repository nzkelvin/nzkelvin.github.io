# Social Impact
## 27/Jan/2020
Many people in Shanghai started to stock up food in case of more city lock downs. Supermarkets are flooded by people. I decided not to go because I think I should avoid all crowded places and panic is unnecessary. 

The central government has announced an extended Lunar New year holiday period to 2/Feb/2020 from 30/Jan/2020. 

The Shanghai city government followed up with another annoucement to ban companies from opening for business until 9/Feb/2020.
